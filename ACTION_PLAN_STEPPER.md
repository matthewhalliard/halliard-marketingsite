# MMM Action Plan Stepper ‚Äì Brainstorm

## 1. Purpose
Capture key information about a prospect‚Äôs marketing-measurement situation, diagnose maturity/gaps, and automatically produce:
- A numeric readiness/health score
- A tailored action plan (next steps + recommended Halliard services)
- A strong call-to-action (Calendly link) to book a meeting

## 2. User Journey Overview
1. **Landing page / site** ‚Üí user clicks **‚ÄúCreate Action Plan‚Äù**.
2. **Stepper Wizard (5 steps + results)** opens at route `/action-plan` (root-level page).
3. User answers concise, multiple-choice questions (< 2 min total) while progress bar advances.
4. Contact details are collected on the last interactive step (results are gated until email is provided).
5. Results screen instantly shows their score, action plan, and **Schedule Demo** button.
6. Responses and score are POSTed to Formspark, which forwards to HubSpot and Slack.

## 3. Step-by-Step Breakdown

| Step | Theme | Sample Questions | Input Type |
|------|-------|------------------|------------|
| 1 | **Who Are You?** | ‚Ä¢ Are you a Brand or an Agency? | Single-choice radio |
| 2 | **Spend & Channels** | ‚Ä¢ What‚Äôs your average monthly paid media spend?<br/>‚Ä¢ Which primary marketing channels do you invest in? (Select all that apply from *Halliard Media Channels* list below) | Radio + multi-select checkboxes |
| 3 | **Current Measurement Stack** | ‚Ä¢ How would you best describe your current measurement approach?<br/>Options (in ascending sophistication):<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;1. | 4 | **Goals & Timeline** | ‚Ä¢ Primary goal for the next 6 months? (Lower CAC, scale spend, prove channel value‚Ä¶)<br/>‚Ä¢ Desired timeline to solve? (ASAP, 1-3 mo, 3-6 mo‚Ä¶) | Radio |
| 5 | **Contact Details** | ‚Ä¢ Name<br/>‚Ä¢ Work email<br/>‚Ä¢ Company website | Text inputs |
| 6 | **Results & Action Plan** | ‚Äî | Computed output + CTA |

### Microcopy / Tone
- Conversational, jargon-free, friendly.
- Progress indicator: ‚ÄúStep X of 5‚Äù.
- Keep each step to ‚â§ 2 fields where possible.

## 4. Scoring Logic (First Pass)
We score on four dimensions, each worth **0‚Äì25 pts**; total possible = **100 pts**.

| Dimension | Criteria | Points |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Monthly Spend** | < $50 k ‚Üí 5 pts<br/>$50‚Äì250 k ‚Üí 15 pts<br/>$250 k+ ‚Üí 25 pts | 0-25 |
| **Channel Diversification** (count of primary channels selected) | 1‚Äì2 ‚Üí 5 pts<br/>3‚Äì5 ‚Üí 15 pts<br/>6+ ‚Üí 25 pts | 0-25 |
| **Measurement Stack Sophistication** | Ad-platform only ‚Üí 5 pts<br/>MTA ‚Üí 15 pts<br/>MMM ‚Üí 20 pts<br/>MMM + Geo/Hold-out ‚Üí 25 pts | 0-25 |
| **Timeline Urgency** | 6 mo+ ‚Üí 5 pts<br/>3‚Äì6 mo ‚Üí 15 pts<br/>ASAP / < 3 mo ‚Üí 25 pts | 0-25 |

*Brand vs Agency* does not affect the score but is captured for segmentation.

### Score Bands & Messaging
| Band | Range | Label | High-level Prescription |
|------|-------|-------|-------------------------|
| **Foundational** | 0-39 | Early Stage | Focus on clean tracking & basic attribution hygiene. |
| **Progressing** | 40-69 | Building | Optimise channels & pilot MMM. |
| **Advanced** | 70-100 | Maturity | Deploy full MMM + incrementality programme. |

3. On failure ‚Üí show fallback message + still display results (so they aren‚Äôt blocked).

**GDPR / Consent**

**Testing Checklist**

4. Confirm CORS works locally (`localhost`) and in prod.

## 7. Technical Implementation Notes
- **Route:** `pages/action-plan.tsx` (root-level Next.js page).
- **Component Library:** Use existing Tailwind + custom `Button` component.
- **State Management:** Local React state or `useReducer`; consider `zustand` if complex.
- **Stepper UI:** Horizontal progress bar; prev/next navigation.
- **Validation:** Minimal; ensure contact email is work email (no @gmail).
- **Analytics:** Fire event on start & completion.

## 8. Decisions (Previously Open Questions)
1. **Gate results until email is provided:** **Yes** ‚Äî users must supply contact details before the results screen is displayed.
2. **CRM / Data destination:** **Formspark** ‚Äî primary endpoint, with downstream HubSpot & Slack integrations.
3. **A/B test step order or copy:** **No** ‚Äî ship a single flow initially.
4. **Page location:** **Root domain** ‚Äî route `/action-plan` instead of under MMM subdirectory.
